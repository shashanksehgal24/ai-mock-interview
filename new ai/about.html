About

InterviewEase is an AI-powered platform designed to help candidates prepare for their job interviews by providing comprehensive tools and insights. Our platform offers:

  • Skill Gap Analysis: Compares resumes with job requirements and recommends skill-enhancing projects.
  • Mock Interviews: Simulates real interview scenarios and provides feedback on body posture, speech, and responses.
  • Customized Question Generation: Creates interview questions based on job descriptions with adjustable difficulty levels.

Our mission is to empower job seekers with the tools they need to excel in their interviews and secure their desired positions.
